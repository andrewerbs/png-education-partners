Purpose

A coordination tool for evidence-based decisions.

This partnership mapping provides a comprehensive visual and strategic overview of Papua New Guinea's education sector landscape. It identifies key stakeholders, investment flows, and collaborative mechanisms that support PNG's education transformation agenda.

The report is designed to strengthen coordination among development partners, eliminate duplication, and optimize the strategic alignment of international and domestic investments with national education priorities.

Strengthen coordination

Enables NDoE, bilateral and multilateral donors, implementing organisations, civil society, and private sector to align around shared priorities.

Eliminate duplication

Make investment flows and program footprints transparent across all 22 provinces, so resources reach where they're most needed.

Align with national plan

Anchored to the National Education Plan's focus areas and the Education Transformation Vision 2075.

Scope & Methods

Nationwide coverage, validated data.

The mapping encompasses Papua New Guinea's complete education ecosystem, capturing activities and investments across all 22 provinces and the National Capital District — from urban centres to the most remote rural communities.

Geographic & thematic coverage

Activities span every region of the country. Areas of focus are aligned to the National Education Plan and include:

  • Early childhood education
  • Primary and elementary education
  • Secondary & technical education (TVET)
  • Inclusive education & disability
  • Teacher training & curriculum development
  • Infrastructure, WASH, and digital learning
  • System strengthening & policy

How the data was gathered

The mapping exercise used Kobotoolbox for digital data collection, administering structured surveys to education sector partners operating in Papua New Guinea to capture organisational and program information.

Following collection, the team consolidated responses, cleaned and standardised entries, and validated each entry by cross-referencing against known program information. Where gaps existed, supplementary information was gathered from partner websites, annual reports, and program documentation.

This dashboard reflects the endorsed final version — checked by all partners and approved by the NDoE.
