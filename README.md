# PNG Education Partnership Mapping 2025

Interactive dashboard for the National Department of Education (NDoE) Education Partnership Mapping 2025 — the endorsed final report of education stakeholders, partnerships, and key investments across Papua New Guinea's education sector.

**Live dashboard:** https://andrewerbs.github.io/png-education-partners/

## Data source

24 partners across three categories — sourced from the NDoE-endorsed final report (compiled from the 2025 Partnership Mapping Survey by the Project Management & Aid Coordination Division):

- **7 Bilateral partners** — Australia (DFAT), UK (FCDO), Indonesia, Japan (JICA), New Zealand (MFAT), PRC, USA
- **5 Multilateral partners** — ADB, EU, UNICEF, UNESCO, World Bank
- **12 Implementing partners** — BBP, CARE, Catalpa, ChildFund, EPF, KTF, PEAN, Plan International, Room to Read, Save the Children, WINGS, World Vision

## Views

- **Partner Cards** — searchable, filterable grid
- **Table** — sortable comparison
- **Provincial Coverage** — 22 provinces + NCD
- **Analytics** — category, organisation type, focus area, and provincial coverage charts

## Stack

Single-page app, no build step. `index.html` + `partners.js` + Chart.js via CDN.
